How does Hey Ivy match me with the right consultant?

Hey Ivy is all about people. No magic algorithms. No funnny business. Just send us an email with what you're looking for, and we'll show you some students that have been in the same situation as you before.

You only pay after you have selected your consultant and set up a time for your chat. Once you pay, we'll provide you with the link to use for the video chat.
